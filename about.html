About Me


An aspiring game developer with a passion for creating immersive worlds, engaging gameplay, and memorable player experiences. Whether it's through storytelling, mechanics, or design, I love exploring how games can inspire, challenge, and connect people. I’m currently building my skills in game design, programming (Phyton, Javascript, React, C++, Unity, and Unreal Engine), and digital art. I enjoy learning new tools and collaborating with others to bring creative ideas to life. My goal is to contribute to projects that push boundaries and leave a lasting impression on players. In addition to game development, I also have some experience with video editing especially creating AMVs (Anime Music Videos). Working on AMVs has helped me develop a good eye for timing, pacing, and emotional impact, which I aim to carry into my work as a developer, particularly in storytelling and cinematic moments. Right now, I’m working on small indie projects to expand my portfolio and deepen my understanding of the development process from concept to final build. I’m always open to feedback, mentorship, and connecting with fellow developers and creatives.
